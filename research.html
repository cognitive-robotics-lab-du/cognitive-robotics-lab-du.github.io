// AI in Healthcare

Our primary focus is leveraging AI to solve critical challenges in the medical domain. We develop high-resolution deep learning networks for automated disease detection from radiography images (e.g., COVID-19), create systems for assessing stroke rehabilitation, and explore novel approaches for analyzing human activity to aid in diagnosing conditions like Autism Spectrum Disorder.

// Medical Robotics & Computer Vision

We explore the intersection of robotics and computer vision to create assistive technologies. Our work includes designing and developing humanoid robots and anthropomorphic hands for sign language interpretation, utilizing techniques like zero-shot and transfer learning. We also investigate skeleton-based activity recognition and use graph convolutional networks for the precise assessment of physical exercises.

// Advanced Imaging & Machine Learning

Our lab investigates advanced imaging modalities and machine learning techniques. This includes using hyperspectral cameras for agricultural analysis and developing novel methods like color photometric stereo for non-Lambertian surfaces. We apply these foundational vision techniques to enhance data across various scientific domains.
